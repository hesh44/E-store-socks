O nama

Blocks socks started in the spring of 2008. two friends had a vision: to spread happiness by turning an everyday essential into a colorful design piece with a rigid standard of ultimate quality, craftsmanship and creativity. a concept now brought to perfection by the happy socks collective of creators. Today Blocks Socks are sold in more than 90 countries and on every continent. Block Socks features an almost endless variety of models and designs, using a broad spectrum of color combinations and original patterns simply put: there's a pair of high-quality socks and underwear for every occasion, mindset and style.
